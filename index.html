My research interest currently lies in exploring the applications of robotics across various fields such as industrial automation, medical assistance, service support, and entertainment, including game AI. By integrating the latest advancements in robotics technology, I aim to investigate how robots can enhance efficiency and safety in industrial settings, improve patient outcomes and care in healthcare, provide support and companionship in service roles, and create more immersive and intelligent experiences in gaming. I am particularly interested in leveraging cutting-edge research to develop innovative solutions that address the challenges and opportunities within these diverse domains.
